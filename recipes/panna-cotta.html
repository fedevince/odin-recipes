Panna Cotta

Home

Description

Rich custard dessert from the Italian region of Piedmont. A generous serving of panna cotta topped with strawberries is the perfect way to conclude a date night. The recipe is remarkably simple yet delicious and it won't take you more than 20 minutes of active cooking to prepare.

  • 500ml Heavy cream
  • 100ml Milk
  • 100g Sugar
  • 10g Unflavoured gelatin
  • Vanilla bean (or vanilla extract)

Steps

  1. Add gelatin to milk and stir till combined
  2. Pour in a pot heavy cream, sugar and the paste scraped from the vanilla bean, or the vanilla extract.
  3. Bring the pot to a boil on a very low heat
  4. Remove the pan from the heat and mix its content with the milk
  5. Pour the mix into ramekins and, after it cools down, let it rest in the fridge for at least 4 hours or overnight.
