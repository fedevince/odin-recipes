Description

Thinly sliced veal cooked in a rich sauce of lemon and butter make for an irresistible main course. Turkey or other white meats can be substituted for the veal if you prefer.

Ingredients

  • Sliced veal
  • Type 00 flour
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 40g (3 tbsp) butter
  • Salt and black pepper to tast

Steps

  1. Coat the sliced veal in flour and remove any eccess
  2. Melt the butter in a pan on a low flame
  3. Add the veal to the pan and let it cook for a couple of minutes on one side
  4. Add salt and pepper and turn the veal to the other side
  5. Pour the lemon juice and keep cooking on low heat until the sauce thickens
  6. Remove from the flame and serve immediately
