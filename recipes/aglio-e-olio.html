Aglio e Olio

Home

Description

Easy to make and inexpensive recipe, perfect for any occasion. Just four ingredients needed and ten minutes from the time the water is boiling before you can enjoy one of the staples of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Salt it and add the pasta once it gets to a roiling boil. Cook the pasta until shy of al dente (about two minutes less than the instructions on the box).
  2. While the pasta cooks, combine olive, finely sliced garlic, and pepper flakes on a pan on a low heat. The garlic should get golden brown. Before it burns add about half a cup of cooking water from the pasta.
  3. Finish cooking the pasta in the pan with the other ingredients
